Preparation of Giemsa Stain Working Solution for Malaria Microscopy (3 of 7)

Requirements for the Preparation of Giemsa Stain Working Solution

You will need the following supplies, materials and equipment to prepare Giemsa stain working solution:

  • Filtered Giemsa stain stock solution
  • 25 or 50 mL bottle
  • Buffered water solution, pH 7.2
  • Beaker or tube, clean, 5–10 mL capacity for a 10% working solution; 100–500 mL capacity for a 3% working solution
  • Pasteur pipette
  • Whatman filter paper, grade #1
