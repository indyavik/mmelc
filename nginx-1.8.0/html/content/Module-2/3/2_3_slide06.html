Preparation of Giemsa stain Stock Solution (2 of 6)

Materials and Equipment Needed

To make 500 mL of Giemsa stain, you need the following materials and equipment:

  • Giemsa powder, 3.8 g, preferably Biological Stain Commission (BSC) grade, to ensure a standard quality product.
  • Absolute methanol, pure, high grade, acetone free, 250 mL.
  • Glycerol, high-grade, pure, 250 mL.
  • Methanol cleaned solid glass beads, 3―5 mm in diameter, 50―100 pieces.
  • Spatula or measuring spoon.
  • Filter paper for weighing.
  • Graduated cylinder.
  • Glass or plastic funnel.
  • Screw-capped, dark or amber glass bottle, clean and dry, 500 mL capacity (if not available, a chemically clean, dry, clear glass or polyethylene bottle of suitable size may be used, wrapped in dark paper or silver foil).
  • Analytical balance, weighing in divisions of 0.01 g.
  • Shaker, if available.
