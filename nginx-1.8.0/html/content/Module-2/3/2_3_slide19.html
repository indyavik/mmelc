Preparation of 2% KH2PO4 Correcting Fluid

The procedures for preparing 2% KH2PO4 correcting fluid are as follows:

Weigh 2 g of potassium dihydrogen phosphate (KH2PO4).

Add to 100 mL distilled water in a clean beaker. Stir with the spatula until the salts have dissolved.

Pour the solution into one of the glass bottles and label ‘2% KH2PO4‘. Write the date of preparation and name of the person who prepared the correcting fluid.

Store the bottle of 2% KH2PO4 in a cool place away from direct sunlight.
