Preparation of Buffered Solution pH 7.2 (4 of 10)

Requirements for Preparation of Buffered Solution pH 7.2

You will need the following materials and equipment to prepare 2% correcting fluid:

  • Analytical balance accurate to at least 0.01 g (a two-pan trip balance is ideal or use an electrically operated one-pan balance).
  • Filter paper, 11 cm in diameter.
  • Two glass-stoppered bottles, each of 100 or 150 mL capacity.
  • Potassium dihydrogen phosphate (anhydrous) (KH2PO4).
  • Disodium hydrogen phosphate (anhydrous) (Na2HPO4).
  • Distilled or deionised water, about 200 mL.
  • Wooden spatulas.
  • Two beakers of 250 mL capacity.
  • One measuring cylinder of 100 mL capacity.
  • Paper labels.
