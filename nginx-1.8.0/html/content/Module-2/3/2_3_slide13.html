Preparation of Buffered Solution pH 7.2 (3 of 10)

Requirements for Preparation of Buffered Solution pH 7.2

You will need the following supplies, equipment and materials to prepare buffered distilled water at pH 7.2:

  • Analytical balance accurate to 0.01 g (a two-pan trip balance is ideal). Various single-pan, electrically operated balances are also available that are easy to use and suitable.
  • Filter paper, 11 cm in diameter.
  • One glass conical flask, 1 L capacity.
  • One glass beaker, 250 mL capacity.
  • Wooden spatulas (wooden tongue depressors are readily available).
  • Distilled or deionised water, 1 L.
  • Potassium dihydrogen phosphate (anhydrous) (KH2PO4).
  • Disodium hydrogen phosphate (anhydrous) (Na2HPO4).
  • Paper labels.
  • Screw capped glass bottle, clean and dry, 1 L or 500 mL capacity.
