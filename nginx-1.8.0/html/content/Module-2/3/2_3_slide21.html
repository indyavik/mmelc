Preparation of Giemsa Stain Working Solution for Malaria Microscopy (1 of 7)

Freshly-prepared working solution of Giemsa stain, made from well-prepared Giemsa stain stock solution diluted with water buffered to pH 7.2, is recommended to achieve optimal staining characteristics of malaria blood films.

In this topic, you will learn the following two methods of staining with Giemsa stain:

  • The rapid (10% working solution) method
  • The slow (3% working solution) method
