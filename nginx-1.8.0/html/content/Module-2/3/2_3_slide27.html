Preparation of Giemsa Stain Working Solution for Malaria Microscopy (7 of 7)

Quality Control of Giemsa Stain and Buffered Distilled Water

You should perform quality control in the following instances:

  • For every new batch of Giemsa stain stock solution prepared or received.
  • For each new batch of buffered distilled water prepared.
  • Regularly on the Giemsa stain working solution.
  • Lastly, you should record all quality control results in the quality control logbook.
