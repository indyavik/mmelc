Preparation of 2% Na2HPO4 Correcting Fluid

The procedures for preparing 2% Na2HPO4 correcting fluid are as follows:

Weigh 2 g disodium hydrogen phosphate (Na2HPO4).

Add to 100 mL distilled water in a clean beaker and stir with the spatula until the salts have dissolved.

Pour the solution into one of the glass bottles and label it as ‘2% Na2HPO4’. Write the date of preparation and name of the person who prepared the correcting fluid on the label.

Store the bottle of 2% Na2HPO4 in a cool place away from direct sunlight.
