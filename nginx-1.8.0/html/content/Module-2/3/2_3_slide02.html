Selection of Giesma Stain for Staining Blood Films for Malaria Parasites

Romanowsky stains are used to stain blood cells, protozoa and bacteria in blood. In addition to Giemsa stain, there are several other Romanowsky stains in use such as the following:

  • Field Stain (A and B)
  • Leishman’s stain
  • May Grünwald stain
  • Wright’s stain
  • Jaswant Singh Battacharya (JSB) stain
