Slide Filing Cabinets

This cabinet can accommodate hundreds to thousands of slides depending on its size. It consists of stackable cabinets and each cabinet comes with drawers with a writing surface on the front of each drawer. Labelling of storage cabinets is very important for easy identification and retrieval of slides.
