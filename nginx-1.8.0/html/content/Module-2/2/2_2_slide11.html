Preparation of Thick and Thin Blood Films using Finger Prick Blood (4 of 5)

Step Four

With the corner of the same ‘spreader’, prepare the thick blood film by swirling the three drops of blood together forming a circle of about 1 cm in diameter. A circular or rectangular film can be made using three to six quick strokes with the corner of the spreader. The thick blood film is of correct thickness when small print can be read through the film.
