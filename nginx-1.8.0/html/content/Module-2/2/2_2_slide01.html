Unit 2: Preparation of Blood Films for Malaria Microscopy

Introduction to the Unit

Welcome to Unit Two of Module Two. In this unit you will learn how to prepare quality thick and thin blood films for malaria microscopy. Blood films be prepared using either finger prick blood or venous blood anticoagulated with EDTA. You will also learn the correct way of labelling, drying and storing blood films.

Quality malaria microscopy services requires good quality blood films in addition to ensuring that microscopists are competent in reading the blood films.

In this unit, you will cover the following five topics:

  • Materials for blood film preparation
  • Preparation of thick and thin blood films using finger prick blood
  • Preparation of thick and thin blood films using venous blood
  • Drying and storing blood films
  • Demonstration videos on preparation of blood films for malaria microscopy
