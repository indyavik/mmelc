Preparation of Thick and Thin Blood Films using Finger Prick Blood (3 of 5)

Step Three

Prepare the blood films with the slide lying on a firm, flat surface. Do not delay between applying and spreading the drops.

To prepare the thin blood film, place the edge of a clean ‘spreader’ slide at a 45o angle in front of the blood drop. Slowly pull the ‘spreader’ back until it touches the drop of blood and the blood spreads along the edge of the ‘spreader’. Rapidly push the ‘spreader’ forward (away from the centre) in a smooth, continuous motion, so the spreader leaves a ‘feathery’ end to the thin blood film.
