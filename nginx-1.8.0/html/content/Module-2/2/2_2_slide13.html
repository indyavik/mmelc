Poorly Positioned Blood Films

If blood films are not correctly positioned on the slide, they may be difficult to examine. Parts of the thick blood film can be rubbed off by the edges of the staining trough, drying rack or slide frame. You can overcome this problem by using a blood film preparation template which guides you where to place the drops of blood before spreading.
