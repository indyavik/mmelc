Materials for Blood Film Preparation (2 of 2)

In addition to new, clean, grease-free and scratch-free glass slides, you will also need the following materials for blood film preparation for malaria microscopy:

  • Protective latex gloves (powder free)
  • Puncture-resistant sharps container
  • Infectious wastes container
  • Micropipette with measuring range 2―10 µL
  • Pipette tips
  • Standard templates for preparing blood films
  • Drying rack
  • Record forms (malaria register)
  • Lead pencil or permanent marker pen
  • Marker pens and pencil
  • Spreader slide with bevelled corners and ground edges
  • Methanol―absolute or laboratory reagent grade
