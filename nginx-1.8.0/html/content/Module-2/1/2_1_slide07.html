Materials for Venous Blood Collection (Venepuncture)

Other materials for venous blood collection (venepuncture) include the following:

  • Sterile needles―either gauge 21 or 23. The gauge number indicates the bore size of the needle—the larger the gauge number the smaller the bore size. Needles are available for use with syringes, butterfly systems or with Vacutainer® tubes.
  • Syringe—to attach to needles (4–5 mL) if the Vacutainer® system is not being used.
  • Vacutainer® holder if the Vacutainer® system is being used.
  • Tourniquet—an elastic strip used to slow the return of blood and enlarge the veins making them easier to see and feel.
  • Adhesive dressings or bandages—to protect the venepuncture site after blood collection.
