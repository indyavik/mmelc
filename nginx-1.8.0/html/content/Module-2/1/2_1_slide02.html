Learning Outcomes

By the end of the unit, you will be able to:

1. Describe the materials needed for collection of blood specimens.

2. Describe the preparation of patients for blood specimen collection.

3. Describe the safe collection of blood specimens either through finger prick or venipuncture techniques.

4. Describe the safe procedures for storage and transport of blood specimens for malaria diagnosis.

5. Describe the safe disposal of used blood collection devices and other bio-hazardous waste.
