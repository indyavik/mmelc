Module Overview (3 of 3)

Unit Two: Life Cycle of the Malaria Parasite

The second unit covers the following:

  • 1. Lifecycle, including stages in the vector (definitive host) and stages in humans (secondary host).
  • 2. Definitions of key terms such as recrudescence and relapse.
  • 3. Common symptoms of severe and uncomplicated malaria.
  • 4. Diagnostic challenges in severe malaria and additional testing required.
  • 5. Relevance of gametocytes.
  • 6. Laboratory confirmation versus use of clinical symptoms and signs for diagnosis.
