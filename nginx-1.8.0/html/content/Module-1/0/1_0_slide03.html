Module Overview (2 of 3)

Module Content

This module is divided into two units: Overview of the global malaria situation; and Life cycle of the malaria parasite.

Unit One: Overview of the Global Malaria Situation

The first unit provides a brief overview of the following:

  • Global distribution of malaria.
  • Epidemic and endemic malaria zones.
  • Personal and environmental control strategies, including emerging issues.
  • Success of control programmes, with resultant decline in presentations of fever attributable to malaria.
  • Treatment and drug efficacy.
  • Malaria elimination and surveillance for asymptomatic cases.
  • Severe malaria.
