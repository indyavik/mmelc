Module Overview (2 of 3)

Module Content

This module is divided into two units which are: Overview of the global malaria situation; and Life cycle of the malaria parasite.

Unit One: Overview of the Global Malaria Situation

The first unit covers the following:

  • Distribution of malaria globally including occurrence rates in various regions such as Africa, Southeast Asia, Latin America.
  • Epidemic malaria zones.
  • Personal and environmental control strategies as well as new considerations.
  • Success of control programmes, which has led to fewer presentations of fever being due to malaria.
  • Drug efficacy.
  • Malaria elimination requiring surveillance of asymptomatic populations.
  • Severe malaria occurring in higher age groups.
