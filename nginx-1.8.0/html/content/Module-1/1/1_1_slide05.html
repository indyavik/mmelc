Global Epidemiology of Malaria (2 of 8)

The reduction in recorded incidence of malaria is associated with the following:

  • 1. Better availability and targeting of vector control measures, including insecticide-treated bed nets
  • 2. More effective drug treatment
  • 3. Trends away from presumptive treatment in favour of laboratory diagnosis by microscopy or a malaria rapid diagnostic test (RDT) to confirm an infection before treatment
