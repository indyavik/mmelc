Life Cycle of Malaria (1 of 5)

This second topic will refresh your understanding of the life cycle of the malaria parasite.

The life cycles of the four common malaria parasite species are essentially similar. All malaria parasite species have a sexual cycle in females of certain species of Anopheles mosquitoes (primary or definitive host, ‘C: Sporogenic Cycle’ in the diagram below) and an asexual cycle in the liver (A: ‘Exo-erythrocytic cycle’) and blood (B: ‘Erythrocytic Cycle’) of humans (secondary or intermediate host).
