Stages in the Malaria Life Cycle in Relation to Clinical Features

The main clinical feature of malaria is fever, which appears one to two weeks after infection. The classic symptoms of malaria are:

  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Sweats
  • Headaches
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Body aches
  • General malaise
  • Muscle weakness

These symptoms occur when erythrocytic schizonts rupture the red blood cells and release merozoites into the blood stream.
