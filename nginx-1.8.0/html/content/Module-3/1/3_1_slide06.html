Erythrocytes (4 of 10): Abnormalities

Erythrocytes may be deformed in a thin blood film due to poor sample preparation, drying or staining, or erythrocyte abnormalities may occur as a result of various diseases and conditions as described in this section.

Erythrocyte Abnormalities

There are a number of abnormalities of erythrocytes. In this section you will focus on anaemia and congenital disorders of the red blood cell.
