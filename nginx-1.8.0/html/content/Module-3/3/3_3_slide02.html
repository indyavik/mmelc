Learning Outcomes

By the end of the unit, you will be able to:

1. Describe the importance of identifying human malaria parasite species.

2. Identify and differentiate stages of P. falciparum parasites.

3. Identify and differentiate stages of P. vivax parasites.

4. Identify and differentiate stages of P. malariae parasites.

5. Identify and differentiate stages of P. ovale parasites.

6. Describe additional methods to diagnose P. knowlesi.

7. Identify mixed malaria infections.
