Who do you Report to?

Malaria can be a medical emergency and is a reportable disease. The results must be documented on standard documents such as patient health cards (or laboratory requisition forms and work cards) and should be reported to the health professional who ordered the test.

The health professional, usually a clinician, is responsible for informing the patient of the test results; not the laboratory staff.
