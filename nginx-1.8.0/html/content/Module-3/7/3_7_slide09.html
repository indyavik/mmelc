Module Summary

Well done! You have come to the end of this module. Read through this summary to remind yourself of what you have learnt in all the seven (7) units.

In Unit One, you learnt about normal and abnormal blood components; in Unit Two, you improved your knowledge and skills on how to detect malaria parasites in thick and thin blood films; in Unit Three you were able to correctly identify the different human malaria species in blood films; and in Unit Four you accurately quantitated these malaria parasites.

In the final three units you were able to identify non-malaria pathogens; recognise artefacts and contaminants in thick and thin blood films; and lastly recognise the importance and format of quality reporting of blood film examinations.

Congratulations! Feel free to review any materials to improve your knowledge and skills on the topics covered. Take a moment to complete the End-of-Module assessment before you proceed to Module Four of the Worldwide E-Learning Course on Malaria Microscopy.
