Components of a Blood Film Report (2 of 5)

Report Format to Clinicians

The report of blood film examination for malaria microscopy must include the following components:

    1. Positive for malaria parasites or no malaria parasites seen

    2. Species of parasites observed:

    • If the species cannot be identified, malaria parasites can be reported as ‘Plasmodium species’ or ‘Plasmodium spp.’
    • If a mixed infection is noted, all species identified should be reported.

    3. Stages of parasites observed:

    • List stages by species if multiple species are observed.

    4. Quantitation―number of asexual parasites/µL of blood

    • If a mixed infection is noted, a single count of all asexual parasites is typically reported unless otherwise required.

For P. vivax and P. malariae, it is rarely possible to separate asexual parasites from gametocytes with sufficient accuracy to count separately, so the total count can be reported.
