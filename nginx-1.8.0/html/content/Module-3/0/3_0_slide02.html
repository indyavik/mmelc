Module Overview (2 of 3)

Module Content

This module has the following seven units:

Unit One: Blood Components

The first unit covers the morphology (appearance), function and abnormalities of blood cells and structures including erythrocytes (red blood cells), leucocytes (white blood cells) and platelets.

Unit Two: Malaria Parasite Detection

The second unit covers the morphology (appearance) of malaria parasites and practical exercises on how to detect malaria parasites in stained thick blood films.

Unit Three: Malaria Parasite Species Identification

The third unit covers the identification of the five human malaria parasite species; differentiating the stages of each species, including mixed malaria infections; and understanding the usefulness of malaria parasite species identification.
