Thick and Thin Blood Films (1 of 2)

In this module you will describe the difference between thick and thin blood films and the morphology (appearance) of malaria parasites in Giemsa-stained thick and thin blood films.

Examination of Giemsa-Stained Blood Films by Microscopy

Module Two of this course covers standard blood specimen preparation and explains why Giemsa stain is recommended by the World Health Organization for staining blood films for malaria. Therefore all images presented in this course are of Giemsa-stained blood films.
