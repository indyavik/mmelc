Module Overview (2 of 2)

Module Content

This module is composed of two units namely:


Unit One: The Role of Different Tests for Malaria Diagnosis and Screening for Malaria Elimination

The first unit covers methods other than microscopy that are used for malaria diagnosis and case management; the difference between malaria diagnosis and screening; and the range and importance of tests to detect low density parasites for malaria elimination.

Unit Two: Other Methods used in Detecting Malaria Parasites and their Potential Uses

The second unit covers the principles of malaria Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT); how RDTs are increasing access to malaria diagnostic testing; other tests that may be used to diagnose low density malaria infection; and tests used to screen for recent or past malaria infection.
