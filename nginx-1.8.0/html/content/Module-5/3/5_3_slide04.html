Phases of Errors in Malaria Microscopy (1 of 4)

Errors in malaria microscopy can occur in any of the three phases of the diagnostic cycle. The diagnostic cycle consists of sequential processes and their respective sub-processes that begin with patient presentation, and end in reporting and results interpretation for correct patient management. The path of workflow processes are classified into three categories:

  • Pre-examination
  • Examination
  • Post-examination
