Quality Indicators (5 of 7)

Supplies Stock-outs

This indicator reports the number of days in a given period that a particular test kit or group of reagents was out of stock.

Slide Rechecking (validation)

Slide rechecking or validation is a procedure to detect major deficiencies in laboratory performance. Slide rechecking requires correct selection of slides at peripheral laboratory sites and an efficient logistics systems to ensure their transport to the supervisory level for rechecking, according to laid down procedures.

Use of SOPs

The use of laboratory Standard Operating Procedures ensures all laboratory procedures are conducted according to established standards of quality. All laboratory staff performing malaria microscopy should be trained to use the relevant SOPs.
