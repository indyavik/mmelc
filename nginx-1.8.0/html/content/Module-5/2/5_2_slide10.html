Other Quality Indicators

Other quality indicators include:

  • Monitoring productivity of personnel performing test processes.
  • Development and availability of a Quality Manual, Safety Manual and relevant technical SOPs.
  • Daily preventive maintenance of equipment and its relevant documentation.
  • Provision of an effective Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) to manage laboratory data.
  • Documented workplace safety and risk prevention.
