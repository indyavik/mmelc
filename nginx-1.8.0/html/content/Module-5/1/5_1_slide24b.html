Preventive Care and Maintenance of a Microscope
  • Cleaning all objective lenses using appropriate lens tissue
  • Using a soft cloth to clean other parts of the microscope
  • Switching off the light and power after use
  • Turning to the lowest power objective and lowering the stage before removing slides from the stage
  • Cleaning spillage of immersion oil on the stage
  • Covering the microscope using appropriate covers when the microscope is not in use to avoid exposure to dust
  • Taking proper precautions in humid areas to avoid fungal growth on the lenses and inside the microscope
  • Keeping the microscope in its place ready for use, away from direct sunlight and near the power source
  • Protecting the microscope from power surges using a voltage stabiliser
