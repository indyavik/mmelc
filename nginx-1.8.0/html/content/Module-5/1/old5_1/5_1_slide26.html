pH Meter

To ensure accurate and reliable analytical measurements, routine care and maintenance of the electrodes of pH meters is required. During use, the membrane and diaphragm of the electrode can become contaminated, which will result in measurement errors or slow electrode response.

The following are important preventative care and maintenance procedures for pH meter:

  • At the end of each day of use, wipe the surface of the pH meter with a soft cloth soaked in 70% methanol.
  • Soak the electrode in 0.1 M hydrochloric acid (HCl) for five minutes. Remove and rinse well with de-ionised water and soak in 0.1 M sodium hydroxide (NaOH) for five minutes. Remove, rinse again with de-ionised water and store in pH electrode storage solution (pH 4 buffer is suitable for short term storage).
