Process Improvement

The management review should cover the following areas:

  • Changes in the volume and type of work
  • Quality control activities, resources and staff training
  • Suitability of policies and procedures
  • Reports from managers and supervisors
  • Outcome of recent internal and external audits
  • Assessments of external bodies
  • Complaints and feedback from customers and staff
  • Results of inter-laboratory comparisons or EQA such as participation in a PT scheme
  • Corrective and preventive actions
  • Recommendations for improvement
