Equipment Management (8 of 15)

Maintenance

It is also important that you keep an up-to-date inventory log of all equipment in the laboratory. The equipment inventory log should include the following details:

  • Instrument type, make, model and serial number so that any problems can be discussed with the manufacturer.
  • Date the equipment was purchased and whether it was purchased new, used or reconditioned.
  • Manufacturer and vendor contact information.
  • Presence or absence of documentation from the manufacturer, spare parts and maintenance contract.
  • Warranty expiration date.
