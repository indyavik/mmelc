Quality Management System

There are many procedures and processes that are performed in the laboratory and each of these must be carried out correctly to ensure accuracy and reliability of testing. An error in any part of these procedures can produce a poor laboratory result. A method of detecting errors at each phase of testing is needed to assure quality.

Health outcomes depend on the accuracy of your malaria microscopy test results and how you report them. If you provide inaccurate results or report results incorrectly, the consequences can be very significant and may include the following:

  • Delay in correct diagnosis and therefore failure by clinicians to provide correct treatment.
  • Use of unnecessary drugs and their associated side effects.
  • Additional and unnecessary diagnostic testing with waste of personnel time, supplies and reagents.
  • Longer hospital stays.
  • Increased hospital and laboratory costs.
  • Poor patient outcomes including death.
