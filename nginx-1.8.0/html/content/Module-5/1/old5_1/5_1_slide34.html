Information Management (2 of 3)

When planning and developing an information management system, the following are some of the important elements to consider:

  • Unique identifiers for patients and samples
  • Standardised test request forms
  • Logs and worksheets
  • Processes to assure accuracy of data recording and transmission
  • Protection against loss of data
  • Protection of patient confidentiality and privacy
  • Effective reporting systems
  • Effective and timely communication to end users
