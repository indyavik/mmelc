Information Management (1 of 3)

Information management incorporates all the processes needed to effectively manage patient and test data, both incoming and outgoing. An effective information management system needs to be in place to achieve the following:

  • Accessibility
  • Accuracy
  • Timeliness
  • Security
  • Confidentiality and privacy of patient and test information



The information management system may be paper-based, computer-based or a combination of both.
