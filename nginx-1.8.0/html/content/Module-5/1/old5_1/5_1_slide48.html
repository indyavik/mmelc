Process Improvement

ISO 15189 describes a similar set of activities for achieving continual improvement in the laboratory as outlined below:

  • Identify potential sources of any system weakness or error
  • Develop plans to implement improvement
  • Implement the plan
  • Review the effectiveness of the action plan through a process of focused review and audit
  • Adjust the action plan and modify the system according to the review and audit results
