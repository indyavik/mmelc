Equipment Management (7 of 15)

Maintenance

Implementing an equipment maintenance programme that includes selection, acquisition, installation, calibration, preventive maintenance procedures, troubleshooting and repairs requires you to conduct the following:

  • Developing written policies and procedures for equipment maintenance, including routine maintenance plans for each equipment that specifies the frequency at which all maintenance tasks should be performed.
  • Developing formats for equipment records, creating logs and forms and establishing processes for maintaining these records.
  • Training staff on the use and maintenance of the equipment and ensuring that all staff understand their specific responsibilities.
