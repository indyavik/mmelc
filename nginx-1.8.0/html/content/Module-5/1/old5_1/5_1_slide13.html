Equipment Management (1 of 15)

Equipment management is one of the essential elements of a Quality Management System. Proper management of laboratory equipment is necessary to ensure accurate, reliable and timely testing. The benefits of a good equipment management programme include the following:

  • A high level of laboratory performance.
  • Reducing variation in test results.
  • Improving clinician and laboratory staff confidence in the accuracy of testing results.

  • Increasing safety for workers.
  • Producing greater customer satisfaction
