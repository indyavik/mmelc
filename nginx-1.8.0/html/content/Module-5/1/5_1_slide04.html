Definition of Terms

To start, let us begin by defining the terms laboratory quality and quality management system since they are the building blocks of this module.

Laboratory Quality

Laboratory quality is defined as accuracy, reliability and timeliness of reported test results.

Quality Management System

Quality management system is defined as all the coordinated activities that direct and control an organisation or department with respect to quality. On the next slide you will learn about the elements of a quality management system.

ISO is the acronym for the International Organization for Standardization. These standards provide guidance for quality across a range of areas, such as manufacturing and service industries.
