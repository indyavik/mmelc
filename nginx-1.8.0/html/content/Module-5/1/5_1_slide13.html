Equipment Management (1 of 7)

Proper management of laboratory equipment is necessary to ensure accurate, reliable and timely testing. The laboratory must establish an equipment management programme that encompasses the following elements:

  • Equipment selection
  • Purchasing
  • Installation
  • Calibration and performance evaluation
  • Preventive maintenance
  • Troubleshooting
  • Service and repair
  • Retiring and disposing of equipment
