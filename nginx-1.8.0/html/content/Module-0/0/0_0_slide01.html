Introduction to the Worldwide E-Learning Course on Malaria Microscopy

Welcome to the Worldwide E-Learning Course on Malaria Microscopy. This is a self-learning course that will enable you to gain knowledge and skills to carry out quality malaria microscopy in order to attain and maintain quality malaria diagnostic services for clinical and research work.

The course covers five modules. As an introduction to the course, we are going to cover the following: background, purpose, goal, target group, benefits, course structure and duration, learning approaches, payment of course fees, assessment and certification. You will also be asked to take a pre-test assessment before commencing the course.
