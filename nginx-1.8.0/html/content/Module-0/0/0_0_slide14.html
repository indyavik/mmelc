Course Assessment Formats (2 of 2)

Unit Assignments

In some units, assignments and tasks are strategically positioned to assess your ability to use and understand the learning materials. These are interactive and will provide feedback on your performance before you progress to the next section or unit within a module.

Final Course Assessment

The final course assessment is required for participants seeking a Certificate of Competence. The final assessment is administered by a course administrator and participants must register for this assessment.
