Course Benefits

The benefits of taking this course include:

  • Improved competency in malaria diagnosis
  • Increased capacity building of the laboratory workforce
  • Strengthened and effective use of resources
  • Improved quality of patient care through better diagnosis and treatment
  • Support to malaria elimination strategies as part of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)



Please note that expertise in malaria microscopy can be quickly lost if you do not practise regularly. These modules can serve as refresher training if you are away from the bench for long periods or if you only occasionally see malaria positive slides.
