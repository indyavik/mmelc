Summary of virtual microscope controls
  • Changing Magnification:
    • Keyboard: '+' and '-' keys
    • Mouse/Trackpad: scroll wheel or two-finger swipe up and down
  • Scanning:
    • Keyboard: arrow keys
    • Mouse/Trackpad: "drag and drop" or click thumbnail
  • Focusing:
    • Keyboard: 'Page Up' and 'Page Down' or 'A' and 'Q'
    • Mouse/Trackpad: Drag slider or click arrows

This list of controls can be displayed at any time you are using the virtual microscope by clicking on the "Help" button.

Tips for scanning the large virtual slides
  1. Set the magnification to x100.
  2. Move to the upper-left corner of the virtual slide.
  3. Scan to the right, pausing at each field, observing parasites and white blood cells, and adjusting focus as needed.
  4. When you reach the upper-right corner, scan down one field of view, then begin scanning to the left.
  5. Continue scanning in this pattern until finishing your observation or reaching the bottom of the slide.
