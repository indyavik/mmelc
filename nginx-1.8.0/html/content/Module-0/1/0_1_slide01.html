Software Setup and Tutorial Introduction

The Worldwide E-Learning Course on Malaria Microscopy makes extensive use of a virtual microscope to simulate examining blood films through a real microscope. The virtual microscope is used to view virtual microscope slides—images representing a large area of a microscope slide as seen through a microscope. In this section we provide advice on setting up your computer monitor, so you can get the most value out of the virtual microscope, and we provide a tutorial on how to use the virtual microscope.

You can return to this section by clicking on the help button near the upper right corner of the window, then clicking the appropriate link.
