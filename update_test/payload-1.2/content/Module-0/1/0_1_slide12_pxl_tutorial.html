On-screen controls

The buttons immediately to the left of the image can be used to change how the slide is displayed.

  • Objective button: Displays current magnification and allows you to choose pre-selected magnification levels.
  • ZStacks button: Displays when the slide has multiple focal planes.
  • Scalebar button: Toggles the scale bar that appears in the bottom right corner of the virtual microscope.
  • Thumbnail button: Toggles the thumbnail. When scanning through a large slide, it may be beneficial to remove the thumbnail to prevent missing parasites that it covers.
