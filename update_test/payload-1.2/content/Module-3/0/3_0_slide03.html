Module Overview (3 of 3)

Unit Four: Malaria Parasite Quantitation

The fourth unit covers methods used for malaria parasite quantitation; and practical exercises on how to perform malaria parasite counting in thick and thin blood films.

Unit Five: Non-Malaria Pathogens in Blood Films

The fifth unit covers non-malaria pathogens that may be seen in blood films, including Borrelia, microfilariae, trypanosomes and Babesia.

Unit Six: Commonly Found Artefacts in Blood Films

The sixth unit covers common artefacts in blood films that can resemble malaria parasites; how to prevent artefacts occurring; and common errors in blood film preparation which may compromise quality malaria microscopy.

Unit Seven: Reporting Blood Film Examination Results

The seventh unit covers preparation of quality reports and accurate communication to clinicians and other health professionals.
