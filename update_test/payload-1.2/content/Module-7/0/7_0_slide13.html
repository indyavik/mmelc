Microscope Slide Examination

You have completed the multiple choice questions in the post-test. You will next examine five microscope slides using the virtual microscope and provide a malaria diagnosis.

Each slide will have at least one thick blood film and one thin blood film to examine. These are accessed by clicking on the button at the top.

Please fill out the entire form for every sample, indicating whether malaria parasites are seen, what species and stage, and an estimation of parasite density.
