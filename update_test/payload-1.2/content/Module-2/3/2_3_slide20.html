Preparation of Buffered Solution pH 7.2 (10 of 10)

Preparation of Correcting Fluid

It is important to observe the following precautions:

  • It is best to store the buffered water in a cool place away from direct sunlight. The use of a dark bottle or a clear glass bottle wrapped with thick dark paper or silver foil is recommended to prevent bacterial, fungal and algal growth.
  • Constantly check for contamination of the fluids by inspecting for turbidity.
  • Do not keep buffer solution for more than seven days to avoid a change in pH and to avoid contamination.
  • Check the pH of buffered water routinely before use and record in the quality control logbook.
