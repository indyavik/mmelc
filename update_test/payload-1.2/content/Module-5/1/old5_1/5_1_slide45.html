Assessment (2 of 11)

When you are conducting assessments or audits in malaria microscopy, you need to evaluate the steps in the whole laboratory process from the time the patient reports to the laboratory to the time microscopy results are communicated to the end user.

Through the assessment process, you should be able to detect weaknesses in the pre-examination, examination and post-examination phases of the testing process and gather information on the following:

  • Current processes relating to malaria microscopy
  • Standard operating procedures for malaria microscopy
  • Staff competencies and training in malaria microscopy
  • Equipment for malaria microscopy
  • Laboratory environment in the malaria microscopy department
  • Handling of blood specimens for malaria microscopy testing
  • Quality control and verification of results
  • Recording and reporting practices
