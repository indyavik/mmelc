Elements of a Quality Management System

The following are the 12 elements of a quality management system which should be implemented and monitored in the laboratory to ensure quality of laboratory services. These are called the 12 quality system essentials.

  • 1. Laboratory organisation
  • 2. Personnel management
  • 3. Equipment management
  • 4. Purchasing and inventory
  • 5. Process control
  • 6. Information management
  • 7. Documents and records
  • 8. Occurrence management
  • 9. Assessment
  • 10. Process improvement
  • 11. Customer service
  • 12. Facilities and safety



This module will address only quality system essential numbers 3 and 7, which are directly related to the provision of quality malaria microscopy services.

All 12 quality system essentials are important to ensure accuracy and reliability of microscopy test results. A detailed description of QMS is available from the WHO:
http://www.who.int/ihr/training/laboratory_quality/doc/en/
