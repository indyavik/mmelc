Learning Outcomes

By the end of the unit, you will be able to:

1. Define the terms laboratory quality and quality management system.

2. Describe laboratory organisation.

3. Manage laboratory personnel.

4. Manage laboratory equipment.

5. Develop a purchasing and inventory management programme.

6. Develop a laboratory process control handbook that provides information on correct specimen management.

7. Manage laboratory information.

8. Keep laboratory documents and records.

9. Participate in occurrence management.
