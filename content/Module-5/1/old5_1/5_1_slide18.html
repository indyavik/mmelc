Equipment Management (6 of 15)

Maintenance

Preventive maintenance includes measures such as the following:

  • Systematic and routine cleaning
  • Adjustment and replacement of equipment parts at scheduled intervals

For maximum efficiency and prolonged lifespan of your equipment, it is recommended that a set of equipment maintenance tasks be performed at regular intervals such as: daily, weekly, monthly or yearly. These tasks will help to prevent the following:

  • Inaccurate test results due to equipment failure
  • Delays in reporting results
  • Low productivity
  • Large repair costs
