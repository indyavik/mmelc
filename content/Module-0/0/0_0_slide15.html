Course Fees


There is no fee for users of the pilot course. The course fees below are subject to change.

The course offers a flexible payment structure:

Component Fees Comments
Course delivery and access to content $10 plus delivery costs Includes one course USB drive and access to the help desk. Multiple users can share a single USB drive
Final assessment in low-resource settings $100 Required for Certificate of Competence
Final assessment in high-resource settings $300 Required for Certificate of Competence
Certificate of Competence Included in final assessment fee Awarded by national licensing body
