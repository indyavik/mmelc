Welcome to Practical Exercise 3.3.1!

In this exercise we will go through several images of thin blood films, grouped by species of malaria parasites present. We will first show the image alone, and then give you the chance to look at our annotations and notes. While you are not asked to identify the species yourself, please think about the features of the parasites as they relate to the species. Also remember that you can rarely identify species based on a single parasite—our identification was based on a thorough examination of the slide, not on these images alone.
