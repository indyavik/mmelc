Unit 1: Collection of Blood Specimens for Malaria Diagnosis

Introduction to the Unit

Welcome to Unit One of Module Two. In this unit, you will learn about the correct blood collection procedures for malaria diagnosis. You will also learn the safety precautions required during blood collection and safe disposal of bio-hazardous wastes.

Following proper protocols during blood collection and handling is essential for safety. While this course provides information on performing these tasks safely, it is not meant to replace formal traditional training in safe blood handling.

You will cover the following six topics in this unit:

  • Materials for blood collection
  • Safety precautions and infection control
  • Patient preparation for blood sample collection
  • Collection of blood samples
  • Transport and storage of blood samples
  • Demonstration videos on blood sample collection
