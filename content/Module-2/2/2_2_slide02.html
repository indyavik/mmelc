Learning Outcomes

By the end of the unit, you will be able to:

1. Describe how to transfer a blood sample onto glass slides from finger pricks and from blood tubes.

2. Describe how to spread blood to make thick and thin blood films.

3. Describe how to air dry blood films while protecting them from dust, direct sunlight and insects.

4. Describe how to differentiate thick and thin blood films of acceptable quality from poor quality.

5. Describe common mistakes and faults in preparing thick and thin blood films.
