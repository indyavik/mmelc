Preparation of Buffered Solution pH 7.2 (5 of 10)

Requirements for Preparation of Buffered Solution pH 7.2

You will need the following materials and equipment for the checking and adjusting the pH of buffered water:

  • Buffered water in a conical flask
  • The two bottles of correcting fluids
  • A pH meter (portable or handheld) or a pH colour indicator and associated components
