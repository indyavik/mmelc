Module Overview (2 of 2)

Module Content

This module has the following four units. Click each unit name for a description.

Unit One: Collection of Blood Specimens for Malaria Diagnosis
The first unit covers correct blood collection procedures for malaria diagnosis using finger prick and venipuncture, safety precautions required during blood collection and safe disposal of bio-hazardous wastes.

Unit Two: Preparation of Blood Films for Malaria Microscopy
The second unit covers the preparation of quality thick and thin blood films for malaria microscopy using either finger prick blood or venous blood anticoagulated with EDTA and the correct way of labelling, drying and storing blood films.

Unit Three: Preparation and Dilution of Giemsa Stain
The third unit covers the preparation of Giemsa stain stock solution and buffered water at pH 7.2 and how to dilute Giemsa stain stock solution for staining blood films for malaria microscopy.

Unit Four: Staining of Blood Films for Malaria Microscopy
The fourth unit covers staining of thick and thin blood films and how to store and archive stained blood slides.
