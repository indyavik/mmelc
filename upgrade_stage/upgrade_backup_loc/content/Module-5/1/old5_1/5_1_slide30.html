Purchasing and Inventory (3 of 3)

The following are some of the details that should appear in your stock log book or stock card:


  • Name of the reagent or supplies received.
  • Date reagents or supplies received.
  • Name of manufacturer and lot numbers for all reagents, supplies, and kits.
  • Quantity or actual number of reagents or supplies received.
  • Expiration date.
  • Number of reagents or supplies issued.
  • Current stock balance.
  • Name and signature of the person receiving the reagents or supplies.
  • Name and signature of the person issuing the reagents or supplies.





The stock logbook is always kept at the storage area.
