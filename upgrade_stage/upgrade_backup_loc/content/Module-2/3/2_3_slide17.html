Preparation of Buffered Solution pH 7.2 (7 of 10)

Preparation of Correcting Fluids

As you have already learnt, there are two correcting fluids you will need to adjust the pH of the buffered water to the optimum pH 7.2. These are:

  • 2% Na2HPO4 (to make the buffer more alkaline)
  • 2% KH2PO4 (to make the buffer more acid)



You will now learn the procedures for preparing the correcting fluids. You will start with the step by step procedure for preparing 2% Na2HPO4 correcting fluid.
