Scanning through the slide

After increasing magnification, you are only looking at a small portion of the slide. The current region is highlighted in red on the virtual slide thumbnail, at the top right corner of the virtual microscope.

There are three ways to scan through different fields of view on the slide. This should typically be done at x100 magnification.

  1. With the keyboard: Press the arrow keys. (Recommended during slide examination).
  2. With the mouse or trackpad: Click on the image and hold down the mouse button while moving the mouse ("drag-and-drop")
  3. Using the thumbnail image: You can quickly move from one region to another by clicking on the desired region within the thumbnail.

Please set the magnification to x100 and take a moment to scan through the slide using the three methods.
