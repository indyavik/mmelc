Unit 2: Other Methods Used for Diagnosing and Screening for Malaria

Introduction to the Unit

Welcome to Unit Two of Module Four. In this unit you will review the principles of malaria Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT) and their role in increasing access to malaria diagnostic testing. You will also learn more about other tests that may be used to diagnose low density malaria infections, or to screen for recent or past malaria infections, which were discussed briefly in Unit One.
